3. Our 5-Step Construction Process

The foundation of our success lies in the combined 20+ years of specialized industry experience of our founders:

  1. Project Planning & Conceptualization: After clarifying your technical requirements and machine specifications, we draft a customized fixture concept tailored to your specific workpiece.
  2. Design: We proceed with detailed planning using advanced CAD. After receiving your final go-ahead on the 3D model, we initiate the manufacturing process.
  3. Manufacturing & Sourcing: The core manufacturing is managed by Datumgrip. We utilize high-precision in-house processes, while specialized steps are carried out by our qualified regional partners.
  4. Assembly & Quality Gauging: Our well-trained and experienced mechanics carry out the entire final assembly. Finally, each fixture is completely gauged using modern measuring machines.
  5. Final Inspection & Trial Support: The final inspection, including a functional test, is carried out on-site together with you. We conclude with Validation & Trial Support to ensure seamless integration with your production line.
