About US

Precision in Every Hold. Innovation in Every Process.

At Datumgrip, we believe that the foundation of world-class manufacturing lies in the details. Based in Rajkot, we are a dedicated engineering and manufacturing firm specializing in the design and production of high-performance workholding devices. From complex hydraulic clamping fixtures to robust manual systems, our solutions are engineered to enhance stability, reduce cycle times, and ensure the highest levels of accuracy for CNC machining operations.

What We Do

We don’t just build fixtures; we optimize production. Our team combines deep technical knowledge with state-of-the-art technology to solve the most challenging workholding problems.

  • Custom Hydraulic Fixturing: Engineered for high-volume production where speed and consistent clamping force are critical.
  • Manual Clamping Solutions: Durable, versatile designs tailored for specialized or lower-volume machining requirements.
  • Precision Engineering: Leveraging advanced CAD/CAM capabilities and CNC expertise (including vertical machining centres) to deliver hardware that meets exacting tolerances.

Our Mission

To empower manufacturers with innovative workholding technology that maximizes machine uptime and elevates the quality of the finished product. We strive to be the "datum" for our customers—the reliable starting point for every successful project.

Why Choose Datumgrip?

  • Design-Led Innovation: We believe a superior product begins on the drawing board. We identify potential machining bottlenecks and optimization opportunities before a single piece of metal is cut.
  • Engineering-First Approach: Beyond aesthetics, our solutions are rooted in rigorous mechanical principles. We engineer for stability, precise clamping forces, and harmonic dampening to suit modern CNC centers.
  • Quality & Durability: A fixture is an investment. Combining high-grade materials with precision machining, we build tools that maintain "datum" accuracy over years of 24/7 industrial use.
  • Tailored Solutions: We don’t offer "off-the-shelf" compromises. We work closely with your team to develop bespoke workholding systems—hydraulic or manual—tailored to your specific parts and machines.
