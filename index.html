Precision in Every Hold. Innovation in Every Process.

At DatumGrip, we believe a superior product begins on the drawing board. Our engineering-first approach allows us to identify potential machining bottlenecks and optimization opportunities before a single piece of metal is cut. From automotive components to precision aerospace structures, we deliver comprehensive, end-to-end manufacturing packages.

We don’t offer "off-the-shelf" compromises. Backed by decades of combined expertise in CAD design and modern CNC manufacturing, we work closely with your team to develop bespoke workholding systems—whether hydraulic, manual, or specialty non-mechanical—tailored specifically to your parts and machines.

Core Expertise

Hydraulic Clamping

Engineered for high-volume production where speed, zero-leak technology, and consistent clamping force are critical to your automated operations.

Manual Solutions

Rugged, durable designs tailored for specialized or low-volume runs featuring anti-lift technology and quick-change capabilities.

Specialty Workholding

Advanced non-mechanical workholding, including precision adhesive and vacuum systems for delicate, thin-walled, or complex geometries.

Precision Engineering

Leveraging advanced CAD/CAM capabilities and deep CNC expertise to deliver hardware that meets exacting geometric tolerances.
