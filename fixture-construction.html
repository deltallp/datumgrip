Fixture Construction

Precision-Engineered Fixturing Solutions

"From automotive components to precision medical technology and aerospace structures, we deliver end-to-end manufacturing packages. We work in close synergy with your team to transform your workpiece specifications into a custom-designed, high-performance tooling concept."

Step 01

Project Planning & Conceptualization

After clarifying your technical requirements and machine specifications, we draft a customized fixture concept tailored to your specific workpiece.

Step 02

Design

We proceed with detailed planning using advanced CAD. After receiving your final go-ahead on the 3D model, we initiate the manufacturing process.

Step 03

Manufacturing & Sourcing

The core manufacturing is managed by DatumGrip. We utilize high-precision in-house processes, while specialized steps are carried out by our qualified regional partners.

Step 04

Assembly & Quality Gauging

Our well-trained and experienced mechanics carry out the entire final assembly. Finally, each fixture is completely gauged using modern measuring machines.

Step 05

Final Inspection & Trial Support

The final inspection, including a functional test, is carried out on-site together with you. We conclude with validation and trial support to ensure seamless integration with your production line.
