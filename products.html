High-Volume Precision & Automated Integration

Designed for complex machining cycles, our hydraulic fixtures eliminate manual error and significantly reduce non-cutting time. Engineered to integrate seamlessly with your VMC, HMC, 5-Axis, or Turning Centers.

Manual Clamping Fixtures

Rugged Simplicity & Rigid Foundation

When hydraulic infrastructure isn't required, our manual clamping systems provide the same world-class stability with lower overhead. Built with hardened tool steel and ergonomic torque points to ensure operator safety and part security.

Features:

  • Quick-Change Capability: Swap setups in under 2 minutes to maximize spindle uptime.
  • Anti-Lift Technology: Specialized internal mechanisms prevent part "float" during heavy vertical cuts.
  • Superior Durability: Black oxide or hard-chrome finishes provide maximum corrosion resistance against long-life coolant exposure.
  • Best For: Prototyping, low-volume runs, and secondary operation setups.

Specialty & Non-Mechanical Workholding

Precision for the Impossible: Adhesive and Vacuum Solutions

For thin-walled components, aerospace composites, and delicate geometries where traditional clamping force causes deformation, Datumgrip provides advanced non-mechanical workholding. By utilizing high-flow vacuum systems or precision adhesive bonding, we ensure absolute part stability without the stress or distortion of physical clamps. This is the ultimate solution for achieving flatness and 5-sided access on fragile workpieces.
